Download and Install¶

Compatibility of CP2K¶

CP2K is tested and should work on the following compilers and libraries: * gcc-9.3.0 or gcc-10.2.0 * Openmpi-4.0.5 * Intel-19.1.3 or Intel-2021.1 * HDF5-1.12.0 * libxc-4.3.4 * cmake-3.18.5 * fftw-3.3.8

To install CP2K-8.1¶

  1. Download version 8.1 of CP2K.

wget https://github.com/cp2k/cp2k/releases/download/v8.1.0/cp2k-8.1.tar.bz2
